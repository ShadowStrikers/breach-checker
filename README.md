# BreachWatch — Breach Email Checker

Check if your email appears in known data breaches, 
with AI-generated risk analysis powered by Google Gemini.

## Live Demo
[Try it here](https://shadowstrikers.github.io/breach-checker)

## How it works
- Email is checked against known breach records locally
- Only anonymized findings are sent to the AI — never the raw email
- AI risk assessment powered by Google Gemini free API

## Privacy
Raw email addresses are never stored, logged, or transmitted 
to any third party except Google's Gemini API for analysis.
